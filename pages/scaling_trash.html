    Aren't the demon's just references?

    As everyone I'm sure is aware, the Devil Summoner Archetype summons demons from Megaten. And many people dismiss this as just references, which is already strange as this is a player character's branch of Archetype which should already clue one in on importance. But they do indeed make several mentions of previous titles lore to make it clear these games are connected to Megaten proper.

    Here Eupha says:

    "The Elder often says that everything is connected through the Sea of Hearts… Do you think they're lending us their power through the Sea of Heart as well?"

    Here they make mention of 心の海. This translates to the sea of souls or sea of hearts (the english translations of the games uses both interchangeably). This is a pivotel concept in the persona series as all demons, shadows, and humans are born from it and are connected via it even across time, space, and universes. Below is le' epic proof of all that, context for P2 the Red Man comes from a different parallel universe (PQ1 and 2 are also evidence of this but it's less epic).

    All of this is to say that since Metaphor introduces the idea of the Sea of Souls being a thing in universe that it would no doubt have to be apart of the grander Megaten multiverse. As well an explenation of where the demons come from. Which would elevate it above being a simple reference and instead a part of the world building. This is even said in an item description.

    This item states "A staff of one who summons supernatural power from the Sea of Souls."
