    Metaphor connecting to Megaten, what the heeeeeeellll.

    Aren't the demon's just references?

    As everyone I'm sure is aware, the Devil Summoner Archetype summons demons from Megaten. And many people dismiss this as just references, which is already strange as this is a player character's branch of Archetype which should already clue one in on importance. But they do indeed make several mentions of previous titles lore to make it clear these games are connected to Megaten proper.

    Here Eupha says:

    "The Elder often says that everything is connected through the Sea of Hearts… Do you think they're lending us their power through the Sea of Heart as well?"

    Here they make mention of 心の海. This translates to the Sea of Souls or Sea of Hearts (the english translations of the games uses both interchangeably). This is a pivotel concept in the persona series as all demons, shadows, personas, and humans are born from it and are connected via it even across time, space, and universes. Below is le' epic proof of all that, context for P2 the Red Man comes from a different parallel universe (PQ1 and 2 are also evidence of this but it's less epic).

    All of this is to say that since Metaphor introduces the idea of the Sea of Souls being a thing in universe that it would no doubt have to be a part of the grander Megaten multiverse. As well as an explenation for where the demons come from. Which would elevate it above being a simple reference and instead a part of the world building. This is even said in an item description.

    This item states "A staff of one who summons supernatural power from the Sea of Souls."

    Which I feel like, pretty open and shut case by now. BUT since I have the curse of obsession, here's some more stuff.

    The Expanse section!

    Metaphor connecting to Megaten, what the heeeeeeellll.

    Before I really go into the Expanse, I'd like to share a quick piece of information about translations. This will be fun, don't worry.

    The Expanse concept has been given many names English translations, offical and unoffical. Terms include: Underworld, Abyss, Monster World, Dem, etc.

    In the God's tongue, Japanese, it is written as 魔界 or Makai. It can be translated to something like Demon World, I guess since it's so generic they decided to spice it up. For the sake of this I will refer to the Expanse as Makai. Below is some important background information about how it translates human thoughts into big old demons.

    Now stay with me, I have more Japanese realms to talk about. So in the spin off series Devil Summoner, instead of going to Makai like in SMT proper, they invented a new realm called Ikai ( 異界 ) or the Dark Realm in the English translations. Think of it like an overlay in reality that allows demons to show up but still in the same location.

    It's clarified in 'Shin Megami Tensei: Devil Summoner World Guidance,' that the Dark Realm is connecting to Makai.

    魔界と空間が繋がった北山大学には数名の生徒と教師が残っていた。異界化は建物の中だけでなく敷地内全体に及んでいた。 外部との連絡は閉ざされ、歩き慣れたはずの構内は迷宮と化していたため逃げ出すことは出来なかったそうだ。

    "At Kitayama University, Makai became spatially linked, several students and faculty were left behind. The change to the Dark Realm did not stop at the interior of the buildings, but spread across the entire campus grounds. All contact with the outside world was cut off, and what should have been familiar walkways within the university were transformed into a labyrinth, making escape impossible."
